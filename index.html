06 / How we work

From brief to master file,
in four moves.

A lightweight, director-led process tuned for velocity. You stay close to the work; I keep the pipeline moving.

01 · Week 01

Brief & decode

We unpack the goal, the constraints and the audience. I write the creative brief back to you so we know we mean the same thing.

i
02 · Week 01–02

Direction & tests

Moodboard, reference grid, prompt system, first-round generations. You pick the direction, I lock the look.

ii
03 · Week 02–04

Produce

Hero frames, shot list, motion passes. Iterate fast, critique hard, burn down the shotlist.

iii
04 · Week 04

Polish & ship

Grade, sound, deliverables in every ratio & cut you need. Handover doc + license. Done.
